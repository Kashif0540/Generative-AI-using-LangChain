# Lecture 01 — Introduction to Generative AI

> "Every AI Engineer starts with understanding the fundamentals before building intelligent systems."

---

## 📖 Overview

This lecture introduces the field of **Generative AI**, its rapid growth, career opportunities, and the complete roadmap required to become an AI Engineer.

Unlike technical lectures, this session focuses on building the correct mindset, understanding the AI landscape, and planning a structured learning journey.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

After completing this lecture, I can:

- Explain what Generative AI is.
- Differentiate AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Generative AI.
- Understand the AI Engineering roadmap.
- Explain why projects matter more than certificates.
- Build a long-term learning strategy.
