# Key Definitions

## Artificial Intelligence

Machines performing tasks that normally require human intelligence.

---

## Machine Learning

Algorithms that learn patterns from data.

---

## Deep Learning

A subset of Machine Learning that uses neural networks.

---

## Generative AI

AI capable of creating new content such as text, images, audio, video, and code.
