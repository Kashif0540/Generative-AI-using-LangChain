# Flashcard 1

### Q:
What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

### A:
Artificial Intelligence is a branch of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks requiring human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

---

# Flashcard 2

### Q:
What is Machine Learning?

### A:
Machine Learning is a subset of AI where systems learn patterns from data instead of being explicitly programmed with rules.

---

# Flashcard 3

### Q:
How is Deep Learning different from Machine Learning?

### A:
Deep Learning is a specialized branch of Machine Learning that uses multi-layer neural networks to learn complex patterns automatically.

---

# Flashcard 4

### Q:
What is Generative AI?

### A:
Generative AI is a type of AI that creates new content such as text, images, code, audio, and videos instead of only analyzing existing information.

---

# Flashcard 6

### Q:
What is the relationship between AI, ML, Deep Learning, and Generative AI?

### A:
AI is the broadest field.
Machine Learning is a subset of AI.
Deep Learning is a subset of Machine Learning.
Generative AI is primarily built using Deep Learning models.

# Flashcard 7

### Q:
Why has Generative AI grown rapidly in recent years?

### A:
Because of:
- Large datasets
- Powerful GPUs
- Transformer architecture
- Cloud computing
- Large Language Models (LLMs)

---

# Flashcard 8

### Q:
What is a Transformer?

### A:
A Transformer is a deep learning architecture designed to process sequential data efficiently using the attention mechanism. It is the foundation of modern Large Language Models.

---

# Flashcard 9

### Q:
What is Prompt Engineering?

### A:
Prompt Engineering is the practice of designing clear and effective prompts to obtain better responses from AI models.

---

# Flashcard 10

### Q:
What are Embeddings?

### A:
Embeddings are numerical vector representations of data that capture semantic meaning and allow similarity comparisons.

---

# Flashcard 11

### Q:
Why are Vector Databases used?

### A:
They store and retrieve embeddings efficiently for semantic search and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) applications.

---

# Flashcard 12

### Q:
What is Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)?

### A:
RAG combines external knowledge retrieval with a language model to produce more accurate, relevant, and up-to-date responses.

---

# Flashcard 13

### Q:
What is an AI Agent?

### A:
An AI Agent is an intelligent system capable of reasoning, planning, using tools, and executing tasks autonomously.

---
