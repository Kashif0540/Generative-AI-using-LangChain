## Q1. What is Artificial Intelligence?

## Q2. Machine Learning is:

a) A superset of AI

# Advanced Level

## Q11.

Why is learning concepts more important than learning tools?

**Answer:**

Tools continuously evolve, but fundamental concepts remain applicable across different frameworks and technologies. A strong conceptual foundation makes it easier to adapt to new tools.

---

## Q12.

Why should beginners build projects while learning?

**Answer:**

Projects reinforce theoretical concepts, improve problem-solving skills, and demonstrate practical ability through a portfolio.

---

## Q13.

Explain the relationship between AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Generative AI.

**Answer:**

Artificial Intelligence is the broad field.

Machine Learning is a subset of AI.

Deep Learning is a subset of Machine Learning.

Generative AI is primarily built using Deep Learning models.
